Aunty Celen's Watercress Soup

Description

Ingredients

  • 7 cloves of finely minced garlic
  • 5 sticks of imitation crab chopped
  • However many fishballs your heart desires halved
  • 2 bundles of watercress washed and roughly chopped
  • 1 quart of chicken stock, chicken broth, or water
  • Chopped scallions
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • Sesame oil and white pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Saute minced garlic in oil on medium heat. any oil.
  2. When garlic is lightly browned, add chicken broth, soy sauce, oyster sauce, imitation crab, fish balls
  3. Bring to a boil then add watercress
  4. Boil for 2 to 3 minutes
  5. Add scallions, rice wine vinegar, optional white pepper, and sesame oil to finish
