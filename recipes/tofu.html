Aunty Celen's Silken Tofu Pour-Over Soup


Description

Ingredients

  • Your desired amount of ground turkey or pork (Optional)
  • 8 grams of Korean Soup Seaweed
  • 8 grams of dried bean curd skin
  • 2 chopped scallions
  • 3 dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 1/2 a small to medium carrot (shredded)
  • 1 bundle of mung bean starch vermicelli noodles (soaked)
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp granulated sugar
  • 1 to 5 pinches of msg
  • white pepper and salt to taste
  • 1 tsp of sesame oil
  • 1 box (8oz) of silken tofu (thinly sliced)
  • 1 quart of stock or water

Steps

  1. Rinse and soak the seaweed and shiitake mushrooms together
  2. Reserve the soaking liquid for later. Slice shiitake mushrooms
  3. Soak the bean curd skin and vermicelli noodles in separate bowls
  4. Saute scallions and ground meat (if applicable) in oil on medium heat until the meat is cooked
  5. Add carrots and cook for 2 minutes
  6. Add shiitake, bean curd skin, oyster sauce, sugar, soaking liquid, soy sauce, garlic powder, msg, and sesame oil
  7. Boil until the flavors have sufficiently melded
  8. Add seaweed and vermicelli. Cook for 2-3 minutes
  9. Finish with sesame oil
  10. Serve hot poured over thinly sliced silken tofu in a beautiful bowl
